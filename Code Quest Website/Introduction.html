Hi Player!

The year is 4865 on planet Earth (a very distant future, in other words) and the humans on Earth are currently at war with the Bruk, an alien race from Brukenst. This war has been going on for 45 years now, and it's about time it came to an end.
The Bruk are intending to weaken the Earth by destroying its World Wide Web, which is very significant to the humans as it allows communication between people, allows people to expand their knowledge and gives them a place for enjoyment! The World Wide Web is the most efficient form of communication for the humans at this time, so they would struggle to defeat the Bruk without it.

You are a Bruk who is secretly working for the humans. Alongside Professor Web, you must help save the World Wide Web by building the ultimate web page that will defeat the Bruk!
